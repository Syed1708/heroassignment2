Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Our Kinder program is a magical introduction to the world of learning, designed for young minds to explore, create, and grow. Through playful activities, storytelling, and hands-on discovery, children build foundational skills in reading, writing, and math while developing their creativity and confidence. Social skills blossom as they learn to share, take turns, and make their first friendships in a safe, nurturing environment.

Elementary School

The Elementary curriculum builds on the wonder of early childhood by introducing structured learning in subjects like language, math, science, and art. Engaging projects and activities encourage critical thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. Students also begin to explore their unique talents, whether itâ€™s through music, sports, or technology. With guidance from caring teachers, they gain the knowledge and confidence to take on new challenges.

Middle (10-16) Years

The Middle curriculum is designed to prepare students for the future by fostering independence, leadership, and deeper academic understanding. Core subjects like science, math, and literature are enriched with opportunities to delve into advanced topics and creative projects. Life skills such as time management, goal setting, and collaboration are emphasized alongside extracurricular activities, helping students discover their passions and build a strong foundation for high school and beyond.
